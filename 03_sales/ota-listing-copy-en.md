## F. FAQ (English — for listing & website)

**Q. Do I need to know how to surf?**
A. No. JetSurf and Fliteboard are motorized — your power comes from the board, not the wave. Most beginners are riding within 15 minutes.

**Q. Do I need to swim?**
A. Basic comfort in water helps, but a certified life jacket is mandatory and worn at all times. Tell us in advance if anyone in your group is a non-swimmer and we'll adapt.

**Q. Age and weight limits?**
A. JetSurf and Fliteboard: 14+, max ~100 kg. Banana boat and floating platform: 8+. We'll confirm in your booking.

**Q. What should I bring?**
A. Swimsuit, towel, sunscreen, change of clothes. Waterproof phone pouch optional. We provide wetsuits in cooler months and all safety gear.

**Q. How do I get there from Seoul?**
A. Public transit: ITX Cheongchun line to {nearest station}, then taxi or our pickup (subject to confirmation). Driving: ~1 hour 15 min from central Seoul via Hwado IC (20 min from the highway).

**Q. Is hotel/station pickup available?**
A. Pickup from {Sangcheon Station / nearby pickup point} is available — please request at booking. Pickup zones and fees confirmed by message.

**Q. What if it rains?**
A. We monitor the forecast the day before. If we cancel due to weather/water conditions, you get a 100% refund or free reschedule. Light rain doesn't stop the activity — we provide rain gear.

**Q. Cancellation policy?**
A. 48 hours+ before start time: 100% refund. Within 48 hours: 50% refund. No-show: no refund. Weather cancellation by us: 100% refund or reschedule.

**Q. Will I really learn it in one session?**
A. Yes — JetSurf and Fliteboard are designed to be learnable in a single beginner lesson. Riders who've never done any board sport are typically standing, riding, and turning by the end of the hour.

**Q. Can I bring a GoPro or phone?**
A. Yes. We also film your ride with our own HD camera and send you the footage afterward, free with every session.

**Q. Group of 4 or more?**
A. Discounts and custom packages available. Message us with your group size and date.
