# On-site Guide Script (English)

> Spoken script for greeting, briefing, and wrap-up. Keep it warm, clear, and short.

## 1) Welcome (at meeting point)
> "Hi everyone, welcome! I'm {NAME}, I'll be your guide today.
> Before we head to the water, I'll get your check-in done — names, headcount, anyone with conditions I should know about. Then we'll do a quick safety briefing, get geared up, and have some fun!"

**Check-in items**
- Confirm names and party size
- Ask about kids, non-swimmers, medical conditions, pregnancy
- Collect / verify signed waiver if required
- Hand out life jackets

## 2) Safety briefing
> "Quick safety talk — 2 minutes, then we're off.
>
> 1. **Life jackets** — stay on the whole time you're on or near the water. I'll check the fit on each of you.
> 2. **Signals** — if you want to slow down, raise one hand. To stop, raise both. The operator will see you.
> 3. **If you fall off** — relax, float on your back, the jacket does the work. We'll come to you.
> 4. **Stay clear of the engine** when boarding and getting off.
> 5. **Look out for each other** — if you see something off with someone in your group, wave the operator down.
>
> Any questions before we go?"

## 3) During the activity
- Stay visible to the group
- Translate / explain operator's instructions in English as needed
- Offer to take photos/videos from a safe spot
- Watch for distress signals, sunburn, fatigue

## 4) Wrap-up
> "Hope you had a great time! Quick wrap-up:
>
> - Changing rooms / showers are over here {POINT}.
> - We'll send the photos and videos to {METHOD} within {TIME}.
> - If you enjoyed it, a quick Google or Klook review really helps us — I'll DM you the link.
> - If you're up for it, tag us on Insta @{HANDLE} — we love sharing guest moments.
>
> Safe travels, and come back next summer!"

## Edge cases
- **Non-swimmer**: extra check on jacket fit, place near operator, brief separately
- **Kids**: parents in same boat/raft when possible, double-check jacket
- **Language barrier (non-English speaker)**: use translation app, demonstrate physically
- **Injury**: stop activity → first aid → call partner manager → record incident
